My MHPC Project in Brief

Exploit HPC for the calibration of parameters of the GEOtop hydrological model

Challenges: many parameters, no good prior, CPU bounded (cf. ML/AI hyperparameter optimization)

Parallel Derivative-free heuristic algorithms are the only option

Goals

  1. Write a reusable calibration tool, with a simple yet general enough interface, easily deployable on HPC systems
  2. Benchmark algorithms, objective functions, and hyperparameters

Approach/Design Choices

  • Python: simple language, good tooling and wide adoption
  • Modularity, encapsulation and referential transparency
  • Emphasis on documentation and reproducibility
  • Do not reinvent the wheel
    • Deployment: Pipenv, Papermill, Singularity
    • Libs: Nevergrad, SALib, Ray
